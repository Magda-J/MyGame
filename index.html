Angry Mushrooms Game

In the enchanting Fungal Kingdom, Mushroom King returns from his diplomatic mission to a realm besieged by an angry Wizard's curse. Citizens transform into ferocious Angry Mushrooms, and the only known solution is to fight them in self-defence using magical items or kill the Wizard who cursed them.

Mushroom King embarks on a perilous journey through his Kingdom to enter and regain control over his castle.

To clear his path, he battles the enraged mushrooms and seeks assistance from uninfected allies by collecting or fetching objects offered by them.

To explore the Kingdom as the Mushroom King character and engage with your surroundings, you can employ a variety of two-word and one-word commands, such as "south" for direction, or "collect axe" to collect an item. Some commands are highlighted and underlined other you need to figure out yourself.

Good Luck!
