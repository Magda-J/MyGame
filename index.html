Angry Mushrooms Game

In the enchanting Fungal Kingdom, Mushroom King returns from his diplomatic mission to a realm besieged by an angry wizard's curse. Citizens transform into ferocious Angry Mushrooms, and the only known solution is to defeat them.

Mushroom King embarks on a perilous journey through his Kingdom to enter and regain control over his castle.

To clear his path, he battles the enraged mushrooms and seeks assistance from uninfected allies by collecting objectsfrom them and conversing to find out where the infected mushrooms may be hiding.

To explore the Kingdom as the Mushroom King character and engage with your surroundings, you can employ a variety of two-word commands, such as "walk south", "collect axe". All possible commands are typically highlighted.
